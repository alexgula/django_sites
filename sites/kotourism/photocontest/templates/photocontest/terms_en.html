Regulations on photo contest «Year of active recreation in Kyiv region»

  1. Terms:

    1. Travel photo contest "Year of leisure in the Kiev region" (hereinafter - Photo Contest) is held by municipal institution of Kyiv oblast Rada «Kyiv regional center of tourism development».
    2. The Photo Contest is aimed at amateur photographers and professional photographers and also all those who involved in photography and has its own travel photos on topics that reflect the tourism potential of the Kyiv region.
    3. This regulations on Photo Contest «Year of active recreation in Kyiv region (hereinafter - Regulations) defines the competitive selection process and regulate issues that arise during its implementation.

  2. Aim of the Photo Contest:

    • creating an attractive image of the Kyiv region and its historical and cultural tourism potential;
    • to attract public attention to the historical and cultural heritage and new recreation opportunities in the Kyiv region;
    • promotion of the tourist centers of Kyiv region;
    • attract the attention of the general public and government agencies to the historical, cultural and tourist potential of Kyiv region, that need urgent attention of issues;
    • emphasizing the positive interest of Ukrainian citizens to the tourism potential of the Kyiv region;
    • enhance the prestige and image of Ukrainian tourism.

  3. Participants of the Photo Contest:

    1. In the Photo Contest may participate professional photographers and amateur photographers who have photographs on tourism topics.
    2. On the contest are accepted work of tourism topics that reflect active recreatin in Kiyv region (during festivals, fairs, holidays and other events).
    3. If you want to participate in the Photo Contest need to register on the official travel site of Kyiv region www.ko-tourism.gov.ua (hereinafter - site). Registration on the site is confirmation for the contest and consent to the terms of its implementation, giving consent to the organizers for processing personal data submitted by the participant of Photo Contest. Registration on the website allows you to put your work on the web site of Photo Contest.
    4. Participant must indicate its real name in the registration form on the site.

  4. Requirements for work:

    1. For the participation on the Photo Contest are accepted photos made during the years 2012-2013, owned by participants of Photo Contest. For the participation on the Photo Contest are accepted no more than 10 works from each participant submitted digitally formatted «PNG» or «JPG». If the author has given more than 10 works, the administration of the Photo Contest decides choice to publish in its discretion, but in an amount not more than 10. If the author has already published 10 works, the new works are not published.
    2. At the request of the Organizer original file provided by the author in the form of «TIF» or «JPG» extension at least 72 dpi in the form of electronic files that are sent to the email address of the Organizer, which is responsible for the Photo Contest: reklama@ko-tourism.gov.ua.
    3. Photos sent to the organizers free of charge for non-commercial use and promotion of active recreation in Kyiv region. Photo is accompanied by a short name.
    4. Conditions of the Photo Contest do not restrict the choice of topic work: it may be some photographs, picture stories that reflect the tourism potential of Kyiv region, development active forms of tourism.
    5. Work does not include installation with any design software and graphics editors. Participants are allowed only original photos.

  5. Conditions of implementation:

    1. The contest will be held from 01.06.2013 till 31.08.2013. Deadline of works - 25.08.2013 (12-00 pm).
    2. Winner of the Photo Contest is determined by public voting at the official tourism site of Kyiv region. For photos can vote only once a day.
    3. Winners is considered a participant whose photo has the most votes.
    4. The winner of the Photo Contest is awarded by prizes.
    5. Nominations: three best work of the Photo Contest will receive consolation prizes.
    6. Fifteen of the best works of participants will be presented on the official travel site «Kyiv oblast for tourists» and in the manufacture of tourism calendar printing products travel topics.
    7. The cost and size of security and prizes will be announced further on during the Photo Contest.
    8. On the Photo Contest is a function of "Like" for social networking. Participant of the photos has the greatest commitment to social networking audience will get a special prize from the organizers. More information about the special prize will be announced on the site during the photo contest.
    9. If you have any problems and technical issues associated with the use of features on the web site of Photo Contest, please contact us in the form of a letter to the email address of the Organizer who is responsible for Contest: reklama@ko-tourism.gov.ua.

  6. Prizes:

    1. Main prize — GPS navigator Prology iMap-7020M, by Photo Contest partner «Auto Sound Base».
    2. Prize — sleeping bag "Pharaon", by Photo Contest partner «Ukrainian Adventure and Ecotourism Association».
    3. Social prize — invitation for two persons to "Park Kyiv Rus", by Photo Contest partner «Park Kyiv Rus».
